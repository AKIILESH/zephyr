Cotton

At Zephyr, we use premium bio-washed cotton that offers exceptional softness and long-lasting color. Our fabric is treated to prevent shrinkage, ensuring that every garment retains its perfect fit even after multiple washes. This high-quality cotton is breathable, durable, and ideal for everyday wear, reflecting our commitment to both style and sustainability.

Polyester

At Zephyr, we use high-grade polyester that is lightweight, durable, and wrinkle-resistant, making it perfect for active and everyday wear. Our polyester fabrics are processed to ensure minimal shrinkage and maintain their shape over time. With excellent color retention and quick-dry properties, they offer both style and practicality without compromising on comfort.
